Music Projects

I've been making music for over 10 years now under the name Cyborg See Cyborg Do.

Some of my tracks have been used as video game soundtracks: for instance, When Words Are Drowned Out was recently used in the game Book of Adventum by Dark Amber Softworks.

My music mostly leans towards atmospheric electronic styles (ambient, drone, noise, etc.).

I've got a lot of experience in sound recording and processing, as well as some knowledge of non-musical sound design.

I'm open to audio-related freelance jobs.
